# About Me

I am a technical professional with over 20 years of experience supporting enterprise technology, cloud systems, integrations, customer-facing technical work, and technical operations. Over the years, I have interviewed candidates, helped evaluate technical skill sets, supported hiring efforts, written assessments, and partnered with teams on technical talent needs.

My background allows me to approach recruiting differently from many traditional recruiters. I understand the language of engineering, cloud, support, and SaaS teams, which helps me better assess candidate alignment and communicate effectively with hiring managers.
