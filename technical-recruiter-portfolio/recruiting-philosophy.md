# Recruiting Philosophy

I believe strong technical recruiting is not just about matching keywords. It is about understanding the role, aligning with the hiring manager, evaluating real capability, and creating a strong candidate experience.

My approach focuses on:

- Understanding what success looks like in the role
- Separating must-have skills from trainable skills
- Screening for problem-solving and communication, not just tools
- Respecting the candidate's time
- Creating clear and consistent hiring processes
- Helping hiring teams make better decisions through structured evaluation

I believe the best recruiters act as strategic partners who improve hiring quality, not just fill seats.
