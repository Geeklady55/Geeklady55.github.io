# Case Study: Filling a Difficult Technical Role

## Objective

Support the hiring process for a difficult-to-fill technical role requiring strong cloud knowledge, troubleshooting ability, customer communication skills, and experience working across engineering and support teams.

## Challenges

- Competitive market
- Candidates often had only part of the needed skill set
- Hiring managers wanted strong technical depth and communication ability
- Role required both technical and customer-facing strengths

## Approach

- Clarified must-have vs preferred skills
- Focused on transferable skills, not just exact title matches
- Used targeted screening questions
- Evaluated candidate communication as part of technical screening
- Emphasized structured feedback

## Screening Focus

- Cloud and infrastructure knowledge
- Troubleshooting mindset
- Ability to explain technical issues clearly
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Customer-facing professionalism

## Outcome

This approach improved candidate quality and helped narrow the funnel toward candidates with stronger technical and communication alignment.
